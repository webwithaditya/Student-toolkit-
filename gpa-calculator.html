How to Use This Tool

To use our GPA calculator, start by clicking "Add Course" for every subject in your semester. Enter the credit hours (weight) and the grade you received for each course. Our tool automatically calculates your Grade Point Average based on a 4.0 scale. You can add as many courses as you need to get an accurate representation of your academic standing. It's an essential tool for tracking your progress.

Benefits for Students

Knowing your GPA is essential for maintaining eligibility for scholarships, sports teams, and honors societies. By calculating your GPA early, you can identify which subjects need more focus and plan your study time accordingly. This free calculator helps you avoid manual math errors and gives you instant results so you can stay on top of your academic goals.

Why Use This Tool

This free GPA calculator helps students track their grades quickly and easily. It works without login and processes all calculations locally on your device for absolute privacy. Students can check their semester GPA and CGPA in seconds using this simple and fast tool. Our interface is designed to be user-friendly, allowing you to focus on your academic success rather than complex math.
