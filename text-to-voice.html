How to Use This Tool

Using our Text to Speech tool is easy. Enter your study notes or any text into the input field above. Select your preferred voice from the dropdown menu (available voices vary by your device/browser). Adjust the speaking speed to your liking using the slider and click "Play". You can pause or stop the playback at any time. It's an intuitive way to hear your notes read aloud.

Benefits for Students

Auditory learning is a powerful way to retain information for students. By converting your notes to voice, you can learn while commuting, exercising, or just resting your eyes. This free online TTS tool helps students multi-task and increases the frequency of academic information exposure, leading to better memory retention and overall study efficiency.

Why Use This Tool

This free text to speech tool helps students convert text to voice quickly and easily. It works without login and supports various languages including Hindi narration.
